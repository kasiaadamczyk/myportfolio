Signage

This project was based on building a new brand and signage for a client. From choosing the colour palette, to typography and imagery I was able to create marketing assets to help my client promote his convenience store 'Sklep Brzezowka'.

Creating outdoor advertising's such as signage and banners creates a subtle yet bright presence stamps the brand into the hearts and minds of potential clients and customers.

The flyers have been created for printing purposes, to announce the store opening and act as a voucher for customers. In addition to indoor aisle and promotion advertising.

Role
Creative Design, Marketing, Brand Development

Technologies Involved
Photoshop, InDesign
