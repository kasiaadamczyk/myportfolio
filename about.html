I Do What I love to do.

Planning, Designing and Developing.

I graduated from Ulster University in 2021, with a first class honours degree in Interactive Multimedia Design. Currently I am employed by Ulster University in the role of a Creative Designer within the Widening Access department.

Throughout the 4 years of my degree course, I have gained experience in development of dynamic websites and web applications using technologies such as HTML, CSS, jQuery on the client side and PHP/MySQL on the server side. During my studies and work experience, I have developed skills in the application of information technology and computer based interactive multimedia. I am highly competent in programmes within the Adobe Creative Cloud and have significant experience in their use to develop logos, images, promotional/marketing content and animation material for my clients. I am highly motivated, hard working and dedicated with a passion to develop a career as a successful web developer.
