Digital and Print Marketing for Manchester United Foundation

Schools Outreach Academy is an e-learning platform which showcases a range of 'Schools Outreach Academy' courses. All courses are available online at Canvas Infrastructure, and previously on Open Blackboard.

Having to develop an identity for Schools Outreach Academy involved building the brand around strict corporate branding rules of Ulster University. Colour theme, typography, imagery and general look & feel required to fit in within the set of corporate rules.

From digital marketing to e-learning course building, print outs, HTML & CSS certificates and promotional videos, I was the main creator leading to building a brand that would be extremly visible and would stand out to pupils aged 8-18, in alignment with the Ulster University brand guidelines.

Role
Creative Design, Branding, Web Development

Technologies Involved
Photoshop, Premiere Pro, InDesign, HTML, CSS
