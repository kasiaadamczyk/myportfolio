Digital and Print Marketing for Manchester United Foundation

As a part of my role, I have designed and developed promotional and educational resources for pupils and external stakeholders such as Manchester United Foundation. I have developed graphics such as e-zine that can be seen below, competition brochures, posters and online banners.

Having to develop graphics for Manchester United Foundation required to have solid foundations around the brand due to strict corporate branding rules which are outlined in Manchester United Branding Booklet. Exact pantone of red, specific fonts only, imagery (required knowledge of the current players of Manchester United as using images with old players is prohibited) and general look required to fit in within the set of corporate rules.

One of the most recent projects includes the three role models (image 2) which will be placed within Magee Campus to inspire other students.
